7. Incidents and Process Improvement

Eight supervisor-fault incidents recorded (Jul–Oct), total cost $4,553.24.

Average cost per incident ≈ $168.21 (excluding outlier 4E260 = $3,375.74).

Root cause:

Incomplete mapping and protocol non-compliance (notably case 4E260).

Action Plan:

  1. Integrate mapping specialist for retraining and QA.
  2. Expand voice-channel coordination and real-time cross-checks.
  3. Extend the Autonomous Controller model to reduce supervisor-related incidents.

As of November 11, 2025, we maintain strict compliance with supervisory protocols, and no accidents attributable to the supervisor have been reported in the past three weeks.

8. WCS (Wireless Charging Stations) Project Performance

  • Improved system stability and readiness for multi-campus rollout.

9. Autonomy Expansion Results

  • Operational consistency and strong performance across AC campuses.
  • Expansion in RLR and CRY; HWU in progress.
